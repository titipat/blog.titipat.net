How to clean install OS X 10.7 (Lion) in 2020

OS X 10.7 codename Lion was released back in 2012. In order to clean install, if your device is 2011 version or newer you can get it done with an internet recovery mode. However, if you are in university or cafes where user authentication is required, USB installer is your choice.

Requirement

  • OS X 10.7 installation media. To be honest, you can find it in “places” like “pirate bays”.
  • Workable Mac device.
  • USB thumb drive.

Step 1

Double click on your downloaded ISO file and it will be mounted on your mac.

Step 2

Open “Disk Utility” tool. Then select your USB thumb drive. Click erase with “os x journaled” and “GUID” options.

Step 3

Select your erased USB thumb drive and click restore. Then select your mounted ISO as an image.

Step 4

Once the Disk Utility completed the restore process. Plug your USB into your Mac. Hold the “alt” key after your heard chime until a yellow hard disk symbol appears, release “alt” key and select the yellow hard disk as a boot device.

Step 5

Complete the installation by following this guide https://www.howtogeek.com/186860/how-to-reinstall-os-x-on-your-mac-wipe-your-drive-and-create-usb-installation-media/
